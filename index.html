Nepal History Timeline

This is an open source movement to collect and visualise historical events of Nepal. We want to create a databse of important events of Nepalese history and present it in various formats so that users can learn about the history of Nepal in various interactive ways. You can support us by providing the details of historical events you know, reporting the incorrect event details etc.
